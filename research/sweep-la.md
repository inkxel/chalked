# sweep.la — LA-metro prior art

**[sweep.la](https://sweep.la)** ("Sweep LA") covers not just the city of LA but **5 separate municipalities**: Los Angeles, Santa Monica, Glendale, West Hollywood, and Pasadena — each running its own independent sweeping program, aggregated into one lookup. Multilingual (en/es/ko/ru/tl), PWA, "as-is, not affiliated with the City of Los Angeles." No GitHub link found on the site — not confirmed open source, unlike [CURB](curb-guide.md).

**Real signal:** crossing city boundaries is already happening at solo/small-team scale in this exact space. Two independent single-city-metro tools (CURB, sweep.la) both exist and both work. The gap they leave — nationwide reach, open source, and the crime-risk overlay — is still wide open.

Individual data sources for Santa Monica, Glendale, West Hollywood, and Pasadena have not been independently traced yet (see the coverage list in the README) — sweep.la aggregates them, but ParkPulse hasn't confirmed its own sourcing for these cities specifically.
